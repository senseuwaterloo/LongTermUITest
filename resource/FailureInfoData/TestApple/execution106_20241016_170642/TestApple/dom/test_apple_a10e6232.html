    Apple Intelligence

    Personal, private, powerful.

    Tap into smart.

    The latest iPad Pro, iPad Air, and iPad mini models are built for Apple Intelligence, which helps you write, express yourself, and get things done effortlessly.1 With groundbreaking privacy protections, it gives you peace of mind that no one else can access your data — not even Apple.

    Find just the right words.

    Writing Tools can proofread your text and rewrite different versions until the tone is just right, and summarize selected text with a tap. They’re available nearly everywhere you write, including third-party apps.

    Express yourself visually.

    Turn a rough sketch into a related image that complements your notes with Image Wand, make a Genmoji right in the keyboard to match any conversation, and more.

    A new era for Siri.

    Awareness of your personal context enables Siri to help you in ways that are unique to you. Can't find the name of a book a friend recommended? Siri can help you find what you’re looking for, without compromising your privacy.

    Designed to protect your privacy at every step.

    With Private Cloud Compute, Apple Intelligence can draw on larger Apple‑designed server‑based models, running on Apple silicon, to handle more complex requests while protecting your privacy.

    Productivity

    Your workplace
    can
    be any place.

    Versatility comes standard.

    There’s never been a more joyful way of getting stuff done than with iPad. Designed for Multi-Touch, simple gestures help you get around smoothly and quickly. Add an Apple Pencil to write and draw your ideas or attach a keyboard to rip through your inbox.2 And do it all with the game-changing performance of Apple silicon.

    Work from anywhere.

    Connect with family, friends, and your team wherever you go. Speedy Wi-Fi lets you download, upload, and transfer whatever documents, folders, and files you need from just about anywhere. And with 5G, you can stay connected even when you’re away from Wi-Fi.3

    Store what’s important. Get to it with ease.

    Organize, share, and get to all your stuff with the Files app. And easily access and manage what’s on your iPad, in iCloud, or on cloud services like Box.

    Creativity

    Take your inner
    artist
    out and about.

    Your most creative canvas yet.

    With a fully immersive touchscreen, iPad is a canvas that’s ready for you to express your most creative self. From 3D sculpting and composing a beat to painting a masterpiece in Adobe Fresco, iPad is where your most creative ideas come to life.

    Apple Pencil is the ultimate creative tool.

    Apple Pencil lets you easily sketch a new idea in Notes, finish a watercolor in Procreate, or draw up production-ready architectural plans in SketchUp.2

    Take your best shot. And video.

    iPad has powerful front and back cameras that allow you to create feature films, high-quality photos, and other amazing content. Apps like Final Cut Pro and Adobe Lightroom let you shoot, edit, and share — all from one portable device.

    Learning

    Your classroom can be anywhere.

    Learning untethered.

    With fast Wi-Fi and cellular capabilities, built-in cameras help you create powerful connections wherever you are. Join class, attend a lecture on Zoom, tackle a group project on FaceTime, or simply stay in touch with colleagues and classmates from all over.

    Enhance your education with accessories.

    Jump-start your notes and projects with accessories that were made for iPad.2 Grab an Apple Pencil and take handwritten and digital notes or connect a keyboard and make quick work of all your emails, essays, and presentations.

    Apps for discovery and exploration.

    iPad is perfect for learning something new. From step-by-step instructions with Kitchen Stories to zipping through a self‑guided course, there’s an app for whatever — and however — you want to learn, including immersive augmented reality apps.

    Entertainment

    Kick back. Tune in. Game on.

    Always have your favorite content with you.

    iPad is a 24/7 all-access pass to your favorite movies, TV shows, books, and music wherever you are.

    A home theater in your hands.

    With an immersive display, amazing speakers, and advanced display technologies like ProMotion, all your content looks and sounds amazing.4

    Ready, set, game.

    The power of Apple silicon combined with a stunning display makes iPad the ultimate immersive gaming experience. Level up by attaching a keyboard or mouse. You can even connect a controller to create a portable console and play games like Call of Duty®: Warzone™ Mobile.2

    Apple Pencil

    Dream it up. Jot it down.

    Apple Pencil sets the standard for drawing, writing, and note-taking.

    Keep a journal, quickly jot down a grocery list, and take amazing class notes. Want to convert those notes to text? No problem. iPad is ready to capture every thought.

    Calling all artists.

    Whether it’s designing something beautiful in Procreate, brushing up your photos in Affinity Photo 2, or finishing your home project in Shapr3D, Apple Pencil has the ability to bring your creative ideas to life with incredible precision.

    Special features to help your workflow.

    With iPadOS, Apple Pencil feels more like writing on paper. Easily sign documents, mark up screenshots, write in text fields, and even leave a note over an app or screen with Quick Notes.

    iPadOS + Apps

    Everyday superpowers. Built right in.

    Touch. And go.

    Designed for the large Multi-Touch display, iPadOS is the operating system that makes iPad so powerful and easy to use. It pushes the capabilities of iPad further — all using the power of touch.

    Powerful and capable from the start.

    Essential Apple-designed apps like Keynote, Numbers, Pages, and more were created to take full advantage of the unique power and versatility of iPad.

    Over a million other ways to use it.

    Turn your iPad into almost anything — from a math classroom or workstation to a professional recording studio — with apps from the App Store. With over a million apps made just for iPad, there’s something for everyone.
