International Collect on Delivery (ICOD) Fee for US Deliveries Announced

Effective June 2, 2025 UPS will introduce an International Collect on Delivery (ICOD) fee of $12 USD. This new fee will be charged to the U.S. consignee at delivery along with any unpaid duties, taxes, and other fees for the shipment.

This change does not apply if duties and taxes are billed to a UPS account number prior to delivery.

Consignees can also avoid the ICOD fee if they pay the applicable customs charges electronically, prior to delivery, through the UPS  International Pre Payment Application (IPPA).

IPPA can be accessed in two ways:

  1. Shipment payment notification

    • Requires shipper to provide recipient’s contact information (name, email, phone)
    • Recipient must have already accepted opt-in terms for notifications from UPS

  2. UPS.com tracking page results link

Rate Updates Effective June 2, 2025

Effective June 2, 2025:

  • The applicable Zone will change for certain origin/destination ZIP code pairs. Revised zone charts will be available via UPS.com beginning June 2, 2025.

  • Additional Handling and Large Package Surcharges for Zones 7 and above will change to:

    • Additional Handling Weight: $55.00
    • Additional Handling Dimensions (Length, Length + Girth or Width): $38.00
    • Additional Handling Packaging/Other: $31.50
    • Large Package Commercial: $260.00
    • Large Package Residential: $305.00

  • Remote Area Zip Codes in the U.S. 48 contiguous states will apply a Remote Area Surcharge to Ground Saver packages.

  • The Shipping Charge Correction Audit Fee will be the greater of:

    • $1.65 per package subject to a shipping charge correction during the applicable invoice period; or
    • 12% of the total amount of shipping charge corrections during the applicable invoice period.

  • The Over Maximum Limits fee for packages will be changed to $1,775.

  • A new International Collect on Delivery (ICOD) fee of $12.00 will be added effective June 2nd

    • Billed to the U.S. Consignee for international shipments

  • UPS rate updates help to support ongoing expansion and capability enhancements as we strive to maintain the high service levels you expect from UPS.

Updates to Other Charges

UPS is making the following changes to U.S. Export and Imports Paper Commercial Invoice Services.

Effective May 26, 2025:

  • Paper Commercial Invoice Services Surcharge: A fee of $12.00 per shipment and will be applied to the shipper when the commercial invoice is not provided by the shipper in digital form using UPS Paperless® Invoice services prior to the processing of the commercial invoice by UPS.

Effective March 10, 2025, the U.S. Ground Domestic and the UPS SurePost® Fuel Surcharge and the U.S. Domestic Air Fuel Surcharge will change. Details on these changes can be found using the link below:

Read More About the Rate Change

Effective March 31, 2025:

  • Print Invoice Fee: A fee of $5.00 per invoice copy will apply for each printed copy of a UPS invoice sent to shippers or other payors.
  • Check Fee and Wire Fee: Payments made by check or wire transfer on or after March 31, 2025 will be assessed a fee of $25.00 per payment. This fee will not apply to ACH payments.
  • The Late Payment Fee will increase from 8% to 9.9% of the total past due invoice balance, including past due late fees.

Effective May 19, 2025:

  • A Payment Processing Fee equal to 2% of all other invoice charges will be added to each invoice.
  • The Credit Card Surcharge will no longer apply.

UPS continues to offer a range of payment options, with convenient and secure tools that make it easy. More details are available on ups.com/billing.
