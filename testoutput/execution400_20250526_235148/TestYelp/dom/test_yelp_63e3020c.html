William C. wrote a review

PokeMoto

Great spot to get your poké fix while you're shopping around Assembly. While there are loads of food options in the area, this place stands out in it's offerings with fresh fish and unlimited veggies/toppings that makes for a healthy meal. With it's limited space, the seating is pretty much where you would line up if it gets very busy. However, the service is quick and friendly. While the proteins aren't marinated like in many poké spots, they have fresh fish and they have many sauces to enhance the flavor of the bowl. I don't mind that they weigh out the protein portions because at least you know you're getting the standard amount no matter who is scooping your bowl and plus you know food costs per portion are a thing. Who would've thought? I've been to other places that do it "by the scoop" which has some variance to it and when you're hungry there is nothing worse than seeing an employee scoop what looks like a normal portion and proceed to knock pieces out of said scoop. Anyways, the portions are generous whether it is the proteins or the additional toppings (look at that bowl filled to the very brim). They even offer seaweed salad and and crab salad as toppings without any additional costs. I've eaten a lot of poké and have had better elsewhere, but that doesn't knock this place for what it brings to Assembly. I've been back multiple times over the past year and this place has been consistent.
