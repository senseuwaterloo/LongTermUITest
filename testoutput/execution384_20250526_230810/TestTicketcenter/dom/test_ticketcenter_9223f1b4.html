      Wicked Broadway Show / Tour 2014

      The Wicked Musical emphasizes a basic truth of life that good and evil are not absolutes. What looks good from one point of view may not be so pleasing if seen from another angle. Highlighting this theme, ‘Wicked' provides a thought provoking back story to the ‘Wonderful wizard of Oz' and takes the audiences far from the sugarcoated realms of the original story to a stage where truth seems as fictitious as the fiction. Making its debut in 2003, Wicked has grown in popularity around the world with its several international editions.

      The ‘Wicked' Musical is based on Gregory Maguire's Novel, ‘Wicked: The life and times of the wicked witch of the west'. The musical not only provides a back story but also runs parallel to the events in ‘Wonderful wizard of Oz'. The musical basically tells the story from the viewpoint of Elphaba, the wicked witch of the west. The story explains the unusual circumstances, in which she was born, and how she gets the title of ‘Wicked witch of the west'. The musical also acts as a mild shock to those who have been reading the ‘wonderful wizard of the Oz' since childhood as it throws a new light on the original characters. The ‘Wicked' made its Broadway debut in Oct., 2003 and created new box office records. The show has become one of the highest grossing musicals at the Broadway, notching ticket sales of over $1million every week. The ‘Wicked' was nominated for 10 Tony awards in 2004 out of which it won in 3 categories.

      Apart from regular shows at George Gershwin Theatre, NY and Orpheum Theatre, San Francisco, Wicked 2014 tour is currently on. The show is scheduled to visit different cities around the US in an attempt to bring this wonderful story closer to the audiences. Book your Wicked tickets and be a part of the musical fanfare.

      Often times, yes. Ultimately, supply and demand dictate successful sale prices and can be more or less than face value. Our sellers determine the price of the tickets. In most cases, where there is high demand and short supply the tickets will sell at more than the face value. However, if the tickets are for an event during an off season or as the event date approaches there are still several tickets available, ticket sellers will often times lower their prices in attempt to recoup some of their costs.

Tickets will be delivered to the billing address you provide and must match the billing address of the credit card you provide us. Most tickets are shipped via Federal Express. All credit cards are charged upon confirmation/finalization. It is your responsibility to track your package and to be available to accept the package.
